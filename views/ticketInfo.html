Ticket Information



Prices

Children under 2: Free

Children (3-11): $12

Adult (12-64): $15

Seniors 65+: $10

Members: Free





Group Discounts

This applies to any non-school group purchasing more than 15 tickets in a single payment.

Adult (13+) : $13

Children (2-12) : $10

Senior (65+) : $8





School Groups

Admission is free for organized school groups Monday through Friday. Please call our office to plan your Field Trip Day.





Additional Discounts

We offer student, teacher, and military discounts as well.

You will need to provide a valid ID.





Free Admission Day

We offer free admission on the first Monday of every month. You can get your tickets one week in advance by calling our main office.
