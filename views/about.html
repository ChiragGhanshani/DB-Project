About the Zoo

The University of Houston Zoo (UHZ) is the zoo currently maintained by the University of Houston (UH). The UHZ is as diverse as the UH student population. With over 1,000 happy residents who hail from many parts of the world the UHZ is surely a wonderful place for the family to enjoy.
